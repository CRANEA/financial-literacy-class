The Fiduciary Narrative Arch

To engage college students effectively within a 1-hour session, we move away from boring arithmetic formulas. Instead, we anchor the lessons around three core actionable concepts:

1. The Debt Trap

Understanding compound interest when it works against you (Credit cards, deferred interest buy-now-pay-later).

2. Compound Wealth

Turning small modern savings habits into major high-yield opportunities early in their career lifecycle.

3. The Fiduciary Filter

How to safely differentiate true professional advisors from commercial "finfluencers" selling platforms.
